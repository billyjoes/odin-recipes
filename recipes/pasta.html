Shrimp Scampi with Pasta

Shrimp scampi with linguine is the ultimate seafood pasta dish. Works with any pasta; angel hair is less filling.

Ingredients

  • large Frozen Shrimps
  • unsalted Butter
  • extra virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook linguine in boiling water until nearly tender
  2. Melt butter and olive oil in a large skillet
